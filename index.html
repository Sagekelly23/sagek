About

For decades, trans people have been under and misrepresentend in media. Whether that is falling into unrealistic tropes that are inauthenti, or just beign completely disregarded as a demographic, it can be difficult to find media with trans people. Rhapsody is an evergrowing collection of media with trans characters, stories, actors, and musicians.
